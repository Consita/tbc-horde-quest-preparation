# TBC Horde Quest Preparation

With just minimal effort, you can reach level 61, and with the full preparation, you can reach level 63 within 2 hours of TBC launching!

We crunched the numbers so you can keep pretending you did. ☺️

# Features
- Prepare 25 TBC launch quests in your quest log, plus an additional 150+ quests ready to turn in.
- Focuses on the most efficient quest route — not every single quest, but the best ones for turn-ins.
- Step-by-step guidance throughout the preparation process, including prerequisites and item collection.
- Required items and reputation tracking so you can gather everything ahead of time.
- Stay up to date on your progress and see how much experience you get on launch.
- Safety feature: Prevents accidental turn-ins or quest acceptance for TBC launch quests. (This can be disabled in the settings)
